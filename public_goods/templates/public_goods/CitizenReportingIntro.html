Citizen Reporting: Public Infrastructure

Welcome to this experiment on citizen reporting. In this study, you will take on the role of a citizen in a community where public infrastructure (such as roads, bridges, streetlights, etc.) may need maintenance or repair.

As a citizen, you have the opportunity to contribute to a public good by reporting faulty or damaged public infrastructure. Your contributions help improve the overall quality of public infrastructure in your community, which benefits everyone.

The experiment will proceed in multiple rounds. In each round, you will decide how much to contribute to reporting public infrastructure issues. The decisions you make will affect both your own outcomes and the outcomes of other participants in your group.
